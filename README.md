App name: HelpLoop- Turning Everyday Help into a Rewarding Experience

Description:
HelpLoop is a gamified mobile application that connects people who need quick
assistance with everyday tasks to helpers within the community.
The app rewards participation through XP, credits, challenges, and team-based activities 
while also encouraging users to give back through community donations and fundraising goals.
